we can make our website more accessible by the following steps :-

keyboard accesibility
this may be usefule for people with motor disabilities and people with blindness
Content inclusivity
Inclusive content is more likely to be accessible to users with disabilities, such as those who are blind or have low vision.
Color contrast
Contrast and color use are vital for good web accessibility, particularly for those with visual impairments.
Headings
Correctly coded headings with heading tags give a structure and hierarchy to content that screen readers can easily understand.
